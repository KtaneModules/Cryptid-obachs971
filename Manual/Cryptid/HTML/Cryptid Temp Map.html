Map Creation

Take the first 6 characters of the map seed and use the letter to determine which map tile that the module is using. Then place the map tiles in the order you got them in this order: Top-Left, Top-Right, Middle-Left, Middle-Right, Bottom-Left, Bottom-Right. The resulting map should consist of a 2 by 3 layout of map tiles, in a 12 by 9 layout of spaces.
